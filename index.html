Majapahit Hotel

Opened in 1911, Hotel Majapahit is one of the most famous heritage buildings in Surabaya. Surrounded by a charming garden, this colonial-inspired architecture is well-maintained and furnished with a modern interior concept for the comfort of the guests. But nonetheless, you can still take a picture of this stunning architecture in the exterior.

Heroes Monument

Heroes Monument is an icon of the city and pays tribute to the fallen soldiers from the Battle of Surabaya. This monument stands at 41.15 metres tall and has a museum built on the bottom. If you would love to learn more about Surabaya history, why not visit this museum. There are plenty of knowledge, artefacts and photos that are put on display.

Mangrove Park

Mangrove Wonorejo Forest is a recreational mangrove area that is frequently visited by locals and tourists. In an area spanning across 200 hectares, this is a perfect place for nature lovers. You will get to learn about the plants that are grown here and its benefits. You may explore this place either by foot or better yet, rent a kayak to discover more sides to the forest.
